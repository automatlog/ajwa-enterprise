Our Certificates

FSSAI Certificate

Issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India, confirming our compliance with food safety regulations.

APEDA Certificate

Accreditation from APEDA for agricultural and processed food product exports.

DGFT Certificate

IEC registration issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade for import-export operations.

Phytosanitary Certificate

Government-issued certificate verifying that agricultural goods are pest-free and safe for export.

Certificate of Origin (CoO)

Document confirming that the products are manufactured and sourced from India.

Commercial Invoice & Packing List

Detailed invoice and packing list outlining product descriptions, quantities, and shipment details.

SGS / Lab-Tested Quality Report

Independent lab or SGS testing reports confirming the quality and safety of our products.
